# A Note from the Author

Dear Reader,

Thank you so much for choosing to spend your time with this book. As an author, there's no greater joy than knowing my words have found their way into your hands and, hopefully, into your heart.

Your journey through these pages means the world to me, and I sincerely hope this story has brought you entertainment, escape, or perhaps a new perspective. Every reader brings their own experiences and imagination to a book, making each reading unique—and that's the true magic of storytelling.

## Your Voice Matters

If you enjoyed this book, I would be incredibly grateful if you could take a moment to leave a rating or review on your favorite book platform—whether that's Amazon, Goodreads, BookBub, or wherever you discovered this story. Reviews are vital for authors like me, helping other readers find our work and allowing us to continue doing what we love: writing stories for you.

Even a brief review makes an enormous difference. A simple star rating or a few words about what you enjoyed can help this book reach other readers who might love it too.

## Share Your Thoughts

I welcome hearing from readers! Your thoughts, feedback, and even your favorite moments from the book are invaluable to me as a writer. Whether you have insights to share, want to tell me how the story affected you, or have suggestions for future books, I'd be grateful to receive your feedback.

You can send your thoughts to: **sloane.grey.author@gmail.com**

Please note that while I read and deeply appreciate every message, due to the volume of emails and my writing schedule, I'm unable to respond to individual messages. However, please know that your feedback is genuinely valued and helps shape my future work. Your insights often inspire elements in upcoming books and help me grow as a writer.

Thank you again for reading, for your support, and for being part of this incredible journey. Readers like you make every late night of writing, every revision, and every moment of doubt worthwhile.

With heartfelt gratitude,

**Sloane Grey**
